Why Choose FoodBridge?

AI Food Recognition

Simply upload a photo and our AI automatically identifies the food type, making donations quick and easy.

Smart Matching

Our algorithm matches donations with NGOs based on location, preferences, and urgency.

Real-Time Updates

Get instant notifications when your donation is accepted or when new donations become available.

How It Works

1

Restaurants Report Surplus

Restaurants upload details about surplus food through our easy-to-use platform.

2

NGOs Get Notified

Nearby NGOs receive instant notifications about available food donations.

3

Quick Pickup & Delivery

NGOs arrange pickup and distribute food to those in need efficiently.

4

Track Impact

Both restaurants and NGOs can track the impact of their donations in real-time.
